About

Established

Our organization has been established in 2015 here in Stockholm city. With weekly organized meetings we aim to spread positivity, care and love within our comunity and beyond.

Empowerment

The goal is to share information, learn new things and to encourage one another to be proud of who we are, no matter if you're straight, gay, cis-gender, transexual, pansexual cause we are all human beings born to be loved.

Inspirational word from MamaRu :

"If you can't love yourself
How in the hell you gonna love somebody else?
Can I get an amen up in here?"
